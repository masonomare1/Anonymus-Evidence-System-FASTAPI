Why a hash isn't enough

A hash proves a file hasn't changed since you hashed it — not where it came from. We split the claim: integrity from intake (SHA-256 + timestamp + chain of custody) and genuineness (AI corroboration across independent items). We never claim to identify the source.

What we do / don't claim

  • ✓ Tamper-evident from the moment of intake
  • ✓ Cross-evidence consistency, corroboration, plausibility
  • ✗ We do not reveal or verify the source's identity
  • ✗ We do not claim cryptographic proof of origin
